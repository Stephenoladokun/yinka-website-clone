Called to Express the Father's Love

I am Yinka Okeleye, and my life is a testimony of the Father's relentless love. Born into a family in Ogun State, Nigeria, I discovered that my purpose transcends making music – it's about creating encounters where hearts remember they are deeply loved.

“When I first experienced the Father's love during my NYSC in Ekiti State, everything changed. I realized that worship isn't just singing to God – it's responding to His love and helping others do the same.”

My journey from Bowen University to becoming a worship leader has been marked by one consistent theme: learning to receive love so I can give it away. Whether leading worship at RCCG or recording in the studio, my heart burns with one desire – to see people fall in love with Jesus all over again.

The Father's Heart Revealed

My ministry is centered on one beautiful truth - the overwhelming, unconditional love of the Father.

Every song I sing, every word I speak, every moment of worship is designed to help you experience this divine love that transforms everything it touches.
